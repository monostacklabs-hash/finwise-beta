# FinWise Features

## Accounts
Track all your financial accounts in one place.

**What it does:**
- Manage multiple bank accounts (checking, savings, credit cards, investments, cash)
- See total net worth across all accounts
- Track balances for each account
- View credit card utilization and limits
- Add/edit/delete accounts

**Account Types:**
- Checking - everyday spending accounts
- Savings - money you're saving
- Credit Card - credit cards with limits and APR tracking
- Investment - stocks, bonds, retirement accounts
- Cash - physical cash on hand
- Other - any other financial account

**Credit Card Features:**
- Credit limit and available credit
- APR (interest rate)
- Statement balance and minimum payment
- Payment due dates with alerts
- Rewards programs and cashback tracking
- Credit utilization percentage
- Autopay settings

**Backend Support:** ✅ Full API support
- GET /accounts - list all accounts
- POST /accounts - add new account
- PUT /accounts/{id} - update account
- DELETE /accounts/{id} - remove account
- POST /accounts/credit-card - add credit card details
- PUT /accounts/{id}/credit-card - update credit card

**Mobile App:** ✅ Complete UI
- Accounts screen with list view
- Account details screen
- Credit card details screen
- Add account form
- Net worth summary
- Pull to refresh
